# ClearSight — 1–3 Minute Demo Script

## 0:00–0:15 | Problem framing
- “Every year, hundreds of millions of users struggle with color vision deficiencies and low vision, yet most teams never see their interfaces as those users do.”
- “ClearSight is a browser-first accessibility simulator that helps teams find these issues before launch.”

## 0:15–0:40 | Privacy and flow
- “Everything runs in your browser. No upload, no backend, no login.”
- Show landing page controls and mention built-in demo images + upload option.

## 0:40–1:25 | Live simulator
- Upload or load a demo UI.
- Show original preview.
- Point out the simulation strip:
  - Protanopia
  - Deuteranopia
  - Tritanopia
  - Protanomaly
  - Deuteranomaly
  - Tritanomaly
  - Achromatopsia
  - Low Vision: Blur
  - Low Vision: Low Contrast
- Mention this helps teams see likely user experience losses instantly.

## 1:25–2:05 | Contrast checker
- Enter text/background colors.
- Show AA/AAA pass-fail output.
- Explain that this is what causes readability failures even when the design “looks fine” to non-affected users.

## 2:05–2:35 | Accessible alternatives
- Click “suggest replacement palette.”
- Demonstrate copying a suggestion and re-checking contrast.

## 2:35–3:00 | Closing
- “ClearSight gives teams fast visual empathy + concrete fixes in one pass, fully in-browser and privacy-safe.”
- Invite judges to try live and compare results.
